Benefits of Recurring Deposits

  • Disciplined Savings: Encourages regular saving habits with monthly deposits.
  • High Returns: Offers one of the highest interest rates in the market at up to 13%.
  • Flexibility: Choose your deposit amount and tenure based on your financial capacity and goals.
  • Loan Facility: Avail loans against your RD in case of financial emergencies.
  • Goal Achievement: Perfect for achieving specific financial goals like education, marriage, or vacation.

How Recurring Deposit Works

  1. Open a Recurring Deposit account with a fixed monthly deposit amount.
  2. Deposit the same amount every month for the chosen tenure.
  3. Earn interest on your deposits at the applicable rate.
  4. At maturity, receive the total amount deposited plus the accumulated interest.

How to Open a Recurring Deposit

  1. Visit any of our branches with your identity and address proof.
  2. Fill out the recurring deposit application form.
  3. Submit the form along with your first deposit amount.
  4. Receive your recurring deposit passbook.

Required Documents

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Passport, etc.)
  • Recent Passport-sized Photographs
  • PAN Card (mandatory for deposits above ₹50,000)
