MAHILA VIKAS Loans

MAHILA VIKAS Loans

Vaasudev Cooperative Society offers specialized Mahila Vikas loans designed specifically for women-led groups to promote collective economic growth and financial independence. Our Mahila Vikas loan program empowers women to establish and expand their micro-enterprises through group-based lending.

With low interest rates, flexible repayment options, and minimal documentation, our loans are an ideal solution for women's groups looking to achieve financial stability and economic empowerment.

Key Features

  • Loan amounts ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹75,000 lakhs per group
  • Flexible repayment tenure of 1 to 3 years
  • Group-based lending with shared responsibility
  • Financial literacy and business training support
  • Regular monitoring and guidance
  • Incremental loan amounts for groups with good repayment history

Eligibility Criteria

  • Woman Group should be registered and operational for at least 6 months
  • Group should have 5-20 women members
  • Regular savings and internal lending practices
  • Proper maintenance of group records and accounts
  • All members should be between 21 to 60 years of age
  • Group should have a clear business plan or income-generating activity

Required Documents

  • Completed loan application form
  • SHG registration certificate
  • Group resolution for availing loan
  • Group savings and lending records
  • Identity and address proof of all members
  • Photographs of all group members
  • Bank account details of the group
  • Business plan or proposal for the loan utilization
