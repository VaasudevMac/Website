Business Loans

Vaasudev Cooperative Society offers comprehensive business loan solutions designed to help entrepreneurs expand or establish their businesses. Our business loans provide the financial assistance you need to take your business to the next level.

Whether you're looking to purchase inventory, upgrade equipment, expand your premises, or manage cash flow, our business loans are tailored to meet your specific requirements with competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options.

Key Features

  • Loan amounts ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹5 lakhs
  • Providing Competitive interest rates
  • Flexible repayment tenure of 1 to 3 years
  • Minimal documentation and quick processing
  • No hidden charges or prepayment penalties
  • Dedicated relationship manager for business guidance

Eligibility Criteria

  • Business should be operational for at least 1 year
  • Minimum annual turnover of ₹5 lakhs
  • Age between 21 to 60 years
  • Good credit history and repayment track record
  • Valid business registration/license

Required Documents

  • Completed loan application form
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID)
  • Address proof (Utility bills, Rental agreement)
  • Business registration documents
  • Last 6 months bank statements
  • Last 2 years Income Tax Returns
  • Business financial statements
  • Proof of business existence
