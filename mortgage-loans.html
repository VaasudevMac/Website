Mortgage Loans

Vaasudev Cooperative Society offers mortgage loans that allow you to leverage the value of your property to secure funds for various purposes. Whether you need financing for home improvements, education expenses, medical emergencies, or business expansion, our mortgage loans provide a reliable solution with competitive interest rates and flexible terms.

With transparent processing, minimal documentation, and quick approval, our mortgage loans are designed to help you access substantial funds while maintaining ownership of your property.

Key Features

  • Loan amounts ranging from ₹5 lakhs to ₹25 lakhs
  • Flexible repayment tenure of 1 to 5 years
  • Provides loan of maximum property value
  • Minimal documentation and quick processing
  • No prepayment penalties after 12 months
  • Doorstep service for document collection and verification

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age between 21 to 65 years
  • Property should be legally clear and marketable
  • Property should be located within our operational area
  • Stable income source with repayment capacity
  • Good credit history and repayment track record

Required Documents

  • Completed loan application form
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID)
  • Address proof (Utility bills, Rental agreement)
  • Income proof (Salary slips, IT returns, Business financials)
  • Property documents (Sale deed, Title deed, Tax receipts)
  • Property valuation report
  • Legal opinion on property
  • Encumbrance certificate
  • Photographs of the property and applicant
