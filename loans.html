Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our loan services

What documents are required for loan application?

Basic documents required include identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID), address proof (utility bills, rental agreement), income proof (salary slips, bank statements), and photographs. Additional documents may be required based on the loan type and amount.

How long does the loan approval process take?

The loan approval process typically takes 2-7 working days depending on the loan type, amount, and verification process. Gold loans are usually processed within 24 hours, while business and mortgage loans may take longer due to additional verification requirements.

Can I repay my loan before the tenure ends?

Yes, you can repay your loan before the tenure ends. We offer flexible prepayment options with minimal or no prepayment charges depending on the loan type and tenure completed. Please contact our loan officers for specific details on your loan.

What is the maximum loan amount I can avail?

How can I check my loan status?

You can check your loan status by visiting any of our branches, contacting our customer service, or through our SMS banking service. Regular statements are also provided for all loan accounts to help you track your repayment progress.
