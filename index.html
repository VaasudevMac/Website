About Vaasudev Cooperative Society

Vaasudev Mutually Aided Cooperative Thrift and Credit Society Limited is a trusted financial institution dedicated to empowering individuals, especially women, by providing financial support for business ventures. With a strong focus on economic upliftment, the society ensures all loans are granted after rigorous background checks and verification, ensuring responsible lending and financial security for its members.

Our mission is to promote financial inclusion and economic development by providing accessible, reliable, and high-yield financial solutions to our members.
