Double Deposit

Your deposit doubles in 5 years 6 months

Our Double Deposit scheme ensures your investment grows securely and doubles in just 5 years and 6 months (66 months). Perfect for those seeking high growth with safety and flexibility.

Key Features

  • Deposit doubles in 5 years 6 months (66 months)
  • Highest safety for your money
  • Minimum deposit amount: ₹10,000
  • Loan facility available against deposit
  • Option for premature withdrawal

How Double Deposit Works

  1. Deposit a lump sum amount (minimum ₹10,000) in the scheme.
  2. Your deposit will double at maturity in 66 months.
  3. Loan facility available during the tenure.
  4. Premature withdrawal option with applicable terms.
