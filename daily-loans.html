Micro Enterprise Loans

Vaasudev Cooperative Society offers Micro Enterprise loans designed to meet the short-term financial needs of small business owners, vendors, and daily wage earners. Our loan scheme provides quick access to funds with flexible repayment options that align with your daily income patterns.

With minimal documentation and same-day approval, our Micro Enterprise loans are an ideal solution for addressing immediate cash flow requirements, inventory purchases, or unexpected expenses.

Key Features

  • Loan amounts ranging from ₹5,000 to ₹1,00,000
  • Quick processing and same-day disbursement
  • Flexible daily repayment options
  • Minimal documentation
  • No collateral required for loans.
  • Repayment period of 30 to 100 days
  • Transparent fee structure with no hidden charges
  • Option to renew loan after successful repayment

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age between 21 to 60 years
  • Stable business or income source for at least 6 months
  • Valid identity and address proof
  • Residence or business within our operational area
  • Good repayment history (for existing customers)

Required Documents

  • Completed loan application form
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID)
  • Address proof (Utility bills, Rental agreement)
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Business proof (if applicable)
  • Bank statement for the last 3 months (if available)
