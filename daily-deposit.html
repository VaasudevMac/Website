Daily Deposit

Flexible savings option for daily income earners

Our Daily Deposit scheme is specially designed for small business owners, vendors, and daily wage earners who prefer to save small amounts on a daily basis. This scheme offers the flexibility to save according to your daily income while ensuring attractive returns on your savings.

Key Features

  • Deposit as little as ₹500 per day
  • Tenure: 12 months (365 days)
  • Attractive maturity values as per chart
  • Door-to-door collection facility available
  • Loan facility available against deposit

Benefits of Daily Deposit

  • Convenience: Perfect for those who earn on a daily basis and prefer to save small amounts regularly.
  • Flexibility: Deposit as per your convenience.
  • Door-to-Door Collection: Our agents can collect your daily deposits at your doorstep, saving you time and effort.
  • Financial Discipline: Encourages the habit of saving regularly, even in small amounts.
  • Attractive Returns: Earn competitive returns on your daily savings.
  • Loan Facility: Avail loans against your deposit in case of financial emergencies.

How Daily Deposit Works

  1. Open a Daily Deposit account with a fixed daily deposit amount.
  2. Deposit the same amount daily or as per your convenience.
  3. Our agents can collect the amount from your doorstep if required.
  4. At maturity, receive the total amount deposited plus the accumulated interest.

How to Open a Daily Deposit Account

  1. Visit any of our branches with your identity and address proof.
  2. Fill out the daily deposit application form.
  3. Submit the form along with your first deposit amount.
  4. Receive your daily deposit passbook.
  5. Opt for door-to-door collection service if required.

Required Documents

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Passport, etc.)
  • Recent Passport-sized Photographs
  • PAN Card (for high-value deposits)
